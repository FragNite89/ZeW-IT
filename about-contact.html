About ZeW-IT

ZeW-IT is an integrated web platform advancing waste management and recycling by connecting commercial and domestic contributors, operators, and sponsors into an efficient digital ecosystem. Our mission is to raise urban awareness, drive responsible waste practices, and incentivize eco-friendly living through technology and rewards.
